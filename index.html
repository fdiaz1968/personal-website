About Me

I am Professor of Finance and Economics at the School of Business of Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María. My main research interests are Corporate Finance, Financial Markets, Banking and Market Efficiency. I am a specialist in Applied Econometrics, Data Science, Machine Learning and Quantitative Methods for business. I teach courses in Financial Theory, Econometrics and Machine Learning.

I have served as an Expert Economist for the National Economic Prosecutor's Office and as a member of the Civil Society Council of the Superintendency of Banks and Financial Institutions, a body that I chaired during 2017. I am a business consultant on Corporate Governance issues, Asset Valuation and on issues related to regulation and competition. I am a member of the Surveillance Committee of various investment funds, Chairman of the Board of Detacoop, and a member of the Technical Investment Council of the Superintendency of Pensions.
